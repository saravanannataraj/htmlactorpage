Azure Architect is a cloud computing expert who designs and manages scalable, secure, and efficient solutions on Microsoft Azure, a popular cloud platform. Their role involves planning the structure of cloud services, defining the architecture for applications and data, and ensuring compliance, security, and cost-effectiveness of the cloud environment. They often work closely with development, DevOps, and IT teams to oversee the deployment of applications, databases, storage, and networking services on Azure. Additionally, Azure Architects analyze business requirements, select appropriate cloud components, and create strategies for maintaining and scaling solutions as needs evolve.
